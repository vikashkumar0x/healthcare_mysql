## Healthcare SQL Analysis

This project contains SQL queries used to analyze healthcare data and generate important hospital performance metrics.

The queries help extract insights related to patient visits, doctor performance, treatment costs, and lab test results. These insights support better healthcare management and operational decision-making.


Key analyses performed:

• Total number of patients  
• Total doctors in the hospital  
• Total revenue generated from treatments
• Year-wise total patients
• Average patient age  
• Top 5 diagnosed medical conditions  
• Monthly follow-up patient rate  
• Average treatment cost
• Average treatment cost by Treatment type
• Total lab tests conducted
• Total lab test by month
• Percentage of abnormal lab results  
• Average patients handled per doctor

## Business Impact

• Helps hospitals understand total patient volume and visit trends.

• Identifies the most common medical diagnoses.

• Tracks total revenue generated from treatments.

• Analyzes treatment cost patterns for better financial planning.

• Monitors lab test results and abnormal result percentage.

• Helps evaluate doctor workload by calculating average patients per doctor.

The SQL queries used for these analyses are available in the file **Healthcare.sql**.
